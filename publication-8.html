Mass Timber Explorations

Understanding that HVAC and hospital building resources would likely be designed into cassette systems per ZGF’s mass timber hospital white paper, these 1”:1’ models investigated possible roofscape expressions in specific parts of the building—the entry sequence, the patient rooms. The deflection and acceleration rates for the more ornamental designs inconclusively lead the design towards a more standard grid, but the idea remained: can we create a spatial experience using the ceiling plane, even for someone bedridden?
