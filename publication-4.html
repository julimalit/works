Library Pavillion: A Container Of Learning

Movement in the ground and roof plane emphasizes ideals of play and curiosity as the foundation of learning. Within this container, there are varied approaches to learning/different use cases, utilizing the gradi-ents of privacy and enclosure created by the landscape: an open lecture pit, enclosed classroom/meeting/resource distribution spaces, outdoor galleries and tables/seating. Inspired loosely by the nearby Ira Keller Fountain.

Material Exploration

Creating a strong expression from a distance and emphasizing Pacific Northwest roots, mass timber frames the project, with steel-clad wood frame structures built for durability within the glass roof.

1’:1/8” model: basswood, mat board, and 3D printed parts.
