Library Pavillion & Civic Steps - ARCH 484: The New Portland Waterfront - Fall 2025

An urban-scale redesign of Naito Parkway and Tom McCall Waterfront Park in Portland, Oregon, folllowing the Portland Bureau of Planning and Sustainability + landscape architects Mayer/Reed’s Central City Waterfront Urban Design Study.

Master plan and 18-foot 1’:1/32” scale model completed with a team of 16 students over the course of 10 weeks. Individual project: civic steps and library pavilion at Salmon St Spring.
