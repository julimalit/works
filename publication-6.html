High Desert Community Hospital - ARCH 484:

Mass Timber Hospital - Spring 2025

A 250,000+ sqft mass timber hospital in Bend, Oregon. Uniquely addressing vibration and sanitization requirements for a hospital program, with mass timber construction design principles. A 10-week partner project with Snow Folpe.

This project drew on historic precedents of holistic healthcare, organizing programming around greenspaces/light, as well as practical hospital efficiency. True to the culture of Bend, there is an emphasis on the connection to the outdoors, for patients and providers alike.

Autodesk Revit, Rhino, and V-Ray for Rhino.
